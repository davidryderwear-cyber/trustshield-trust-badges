Privacy Policy for TrustShield Trust Badges

Last updated: April 12, 2026

Introduction

TrustShield Trust Badges ("we", "our", or "us") operates as a Shopify application. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our app.

Information We Collect

When you install TrustShield Trust Badges, we access the following information through Shopify's authentication system:

  • Store information: Your store name and domain, used to identify your account and display badges on your storefront.
  • Email address: Your Shopify account email, used for account identification and support communication.

We do not collect any personal information from your customers. TrustShield Trust Badges only displays visual badge elements on your storefront and does not track, collect, or store any customer data.

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide and maintain the TrustShield Trust Badges service
  • Store your badge configuration preferences
  • Communicate with you about your account or support requests

Data Storage

Your badge configuration data is stored securely on our servers. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your information.

Third-Party Services

We do not share your information with third parties. Our app operates entirely within the Shopify ecosystem.

Data Retention

We retain your badge configuration data for as long as you have the app installed. When you uninstall the app, your data is deleted.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request deletion of your data by uninstalling the app
  • Contact us with any privacy-related questions

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date.
